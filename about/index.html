About — Stilllife

STILLIFE spreads small, meaningful messages through physical objects in public spaces. We work with simple phrases that express ways of thinking or being.

These phrases appear on posters, tags, or small items placed in the city. Each message invites people not just to read, but to act. By tearing off or carrying the object, the viewer becomes part of the message.

The name 'STILLIFE' comes from the term 'still object'. But instead of just quiet objects, we focus on how those objects can thrive, representing everyday attitudes and actions.

STILLIFE라는 이름은 정물(Still Object)에서 출발했습니다. 하지만 우리는 멈춰 있는 사물이 아니라, 삶의 태도와 행동을 담은 오브제에 주목합니다.

길거리의 플라이어로 전해진 메시지는 누군가 태그를 가져가는 순간 자연스럽게 퍼져 나가고, 정적인 사물에 담긴 태도는 사람의 손을 거치며 하나의 행위가 됩니다.

Selecting Objects

Objects are chosen for their familiarity and presence in everyday life. They are simple and easy to find, helping people remember the message.

How it Works

The viewer becomes part of the message. By taking and carrying the message. By taking and carrying the flyer piece, the phrase continues its journey and becomes your attitude.
